Urban eye health programme

Unity uplift works in 21 cities urban slums with the objective to ensure comprehensive and sustainable eye health to the urban poor.

National school Eye health programme

The national school Eye health programme, prevents eye disease and vision impairments amongst children age 5-18 in government and government -aided schools.

Social inclusion programme

We promote for inclusion and equal opportunities for individual with disabilities, including visual impairments or blindness,to achieve their maximum potential.
